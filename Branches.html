Laya Seaside

Laya Seaside Hotel is a charming coastal retreat that offers guests a blissful escape by the sea. Nestled along the shoreline, this boutique hotel provides breathtaking views of the ocean from its comfortable rooms and suites, many of which feature private balconies. With a focus on relaxation and rejuvenation, Laya Seaside Hotel boasts an inviting atmosphere, complete with a tranquil spa, a beachfront restaurant serving delicious seafood, and direct access to a pristine sandy beach. It's the perfect destination for travelers looking to unwind, soak up the coastal ambiance, and enjoy the serenity of the sea.

Laya Jungle

Laya Jungle Hotel is a nature lover's paradise tucked away in the heart of lush and pristine jungle surroundings. This eco-friendly retreat offers guests a unique opportunity to immerse themselves in the beauty of the rainforest. The hotel's rustic yet comfortable accommodations blend seamlessly with the natural environment, allowing guests to experience the jungle up close. Activities such as guided wildlife tours, hiking through dense forests, and birdwatching adventures are readily available. Laya Jungle Hotel is the ideal destination for those seeking a tranquil and immersive jungle experience while maintaining a commitment to sustainable eco-tourism practices.

Laya Riverview

Laya Riverview Hotel is a serene and charming riverside retreat that provides guests with a tranquil escape amidst stunning natural beauty. Nestled alongside a picturesque river, the hotel offers comfortable accommodations with scenic views of the water. The ambiance is relaxed and inviting, with lush gardens and outdoor seating areas for guests to enjoy the soothing sounds of the river. Laya Riverview Hotel is the perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful and rejuvenating stay, with opportunities for riverside walks, fishing, and a chance to connect with nature in a tranquil setting.
