Special Events

English Resturant

This charming dining establishment within the hotel combines classic English recipes with modern twists, providing a diverse menu to satisfy various tastes.

With an inviting and cozy ambiance, it's an ideal place for gatherings with cousins and family. The warm and welcoming atmosphere creates the perfect backdrop for relaxed conversations and memorable moments. Whether you're craving a hearty English breakfast, traditional fish and chips, savory pies, or afternoon tea complete with scones and clotted cream, this restaurant has it all.

Sri Lankan Resturant

The restaurant's ambiance reflects the cultural essence of Sri Lanka, featuring warm colors, traditional decor, and perhaps even live music to enhance the dining experience. It provides an inviting and comfortable setting for family get-togethers.

The menu showcases a wide range of Sri Lankan dishes, including aromatic curries, seafood specialties, string hoppers, and mouthwatering street food snacks like samosas and roti. Guests can savor the vibrant and spicy flavors of the island nation while enjoying both meat and vegetarian options.

Japaneese Resturant

The restaurant's ambiance is a blend of contemporary and traditional Japanese design, creating a serene and inviting atmosphere. Tatami mat seating, low tables, and Japanese art and decor enhance the authenticity of the experience.

The menu is a culinary journey through Japan, featuring a wide range of dishes like sushi, sashimi, tempura, ramen, and teppanyaki. Guests can savor the exquisite flavors of fresh seafood, perfectly cooked meats, and a variety of vegetarian options. Don't forget to try some of the restaurant's signature cocktails, sake, or green tea to complement your meal.
